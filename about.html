About

Explore John Muir's Yosemite is an interactive multimedia installation for the web created by composer and multimedia artist-producer, Nell Shaw Cohen. Synthesizing historical texts by John Muir with interactive photography and music, this installation offers a multi-disciplinary, multi-sensory interpretation of John Muir's vision of nature.

The beta version of Explore John Muir's Yosemite was launched in March 2014. Since this website is still in beta, it is actively undergoing improvements. If you have any questions or suggestions, please email nell@nellshawcohen.com.
